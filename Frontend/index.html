About Samanta Portal

Our Mission

Samanta Portal is dedicated to creating a safer, more equitable Nepal by providing accessible platforms for reporting harassment, understanding legal rights, and connecting victims with immediate support. We believe everyone deserves to live free from discrimination and violence.

Our Vision

A Nepal where gender equality is not just a goal but a lived reality, where every individual can exercise their rights without fear, and where technology empowers communities to stand against injustice.

Underreporting

Many victims don't report due to fear, stigma, or lack of access

Lack of Awareness

Limited knowledge about legal rights and protections

Delayed Response

Slow response times can escalate dangerous situations

Fragmented Support

Disconnected services make it hard to get comprehensive help

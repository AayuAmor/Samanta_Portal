Know Your Rights

‡§Æ‡§π‡§ø‡§≤‡§æ‡§ï‡•ã ‡§Ö‡§ß‡§ø‡§ï‡§æ‡§∞ (Women's Rights)

Property Rights

Women have equal rights to ancestral property. The Civil Code 2074 grants daughters equal inheritance rights as sons. Women can own, buy, and sell property independently.

Workplace Rights

Equal pay for equal work, maternity leave (98 days paid), protection from workplace harassment, and no discrimination in hiring or promotion.

Protection from Violence

The Domestic Violence Act 2066 provides protection orders, financial compensation, and criminal penalties for abusers. Sexual harassment in public places can result in up to 1 year imprisonment.

‡§™‡•Å‡§∞‡•Å‡§∑‡§ï‡•ã ‡§Ö‡§ß‡§ø‡§ï‡§æ‡§∞ (Men's Rights)

Parental Rights

Equal rights to child custody and visitation. Fathers can seek custody if it's in the child's best interest.

Protection from False Allegations

Legal recourse available for false accusations. Right to fair trial and presumption of innocence until proven guilty.

LGBTQIA+ ‡§Ö‡§ß‡§ø‡§ï‡§æ‡§∞

Right to Identity

Nepal recognizes third gender. Citizens can obtain citizenship certificates with their gender identity. Supreme Court ruling (2007) affirmed LGBTQIA+ rights.

Non-Discrimination

Constitutional protection from discrimination based on sexual orientation and gender identity. Equal access to public services, employment, and education.

About Samanta Portal

Our Mission

Samanta Portal is dedicated to creating a safer, more equitable Nepal by providing accessible platforms for reporting harassment, understanding legal rights, and connecting victims with immediate support. We believe everyone deserves to live free from discrimination and violence.

Our Vision

A Nepal where gender equality is not just a goal but a lived reality, where every individual can exercise their rights without fear, and where technology empowers communities to stand against injustice.

Problems We Address

Underreporting

Many victims don't report due to fear, stigma, or lack of access

Lack of Awareness

Limited knowledge about legal rights and protections

Delayed Response

Slow response times can escalate dangerous situations

Fragmented Support

Disconnected services make it hard to get comprehensive help
